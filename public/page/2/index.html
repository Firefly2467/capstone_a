The mission

Picture this: You’re working away at your desk job for many years, and one day, may not be today, tomorrow, or even this year, but one day…

You’re unable to reach in certain directions; It hurts to bend over and pick up your pen; Constant surges of pain arise when just sitting or standing for too long; Find it hard to focus on your work

Prolonged sitting leads to bad posture, that increases stress on your neck, back, arms and legs, with increased pressure on your spinal discs and back muscles!
